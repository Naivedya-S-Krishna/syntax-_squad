FAQ

1. What is LoopLearn?

LoopLearn is a platform that allows people to connect, share, and exchange skills. Users can create profiles, list the skills they have and want to learn, and collaborate with others to grow their knowledge and talents.

2. How does skill exchange work?

Users can match with others who have the skills they want to learn and offer their own skills in return. If you don’t have a skill to exchange, you can use points to learn from others.

3. How do I earn points?

You can earn points by teaching your skills to others or sharing skill-related videos and resources on your profile.

4. Can I use points to learn a skill?

Yes! Points can be used to access lessons or resources from other users if you don’t have a skill to trade.

5. Is it free to use LoopLearn?

Yes, signing up and creating a profile on LoopLearn is free. However, some features may require earning or spending points.

6. Can I share videos on my profile?

Absolutely! You can share videos related to your skills, such as tutorials, performances, or progress updates, to showcase your expertise.

7. How do I find someone with the skills I want to learn?

LoopLearn uses a smart matching system to help you find users whose skills align with what you’re looking for. You can also browse profiles to connect with others.

8. What types of skills can I exchange?

You can exchange any skill, such as dancing, cooking, coding, painting, or even soft skills like public speaking. The possibilities are endless!

9. How do I get started?

Simply sign up, create your profile, and list the skills you have and want to learn. Start connecting with others and begin your skill-sharing journey!

10. Is there a way to track my progress?

Yes, you can use your profile to document your skill-sharing experiences and showcase your growth through videos and achievements.
